SD‑DEF: Full Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG)

Below is the SD‑DEF engine as a DAG of nodes (modules) and edges (data/control dependencies), organized from inputs → signals → events → scores → analysis → projections → remediation.

---

1. Top‑level DAG overview

`text
[Asset & Context Inputs]
        ↓
[Signal Collectors]
        ↓
[Signal Normalization]
        ↓
[Degradation Event Construction]
        ↓
[Control & Framework Mapping]
        ↓
[Scoring Engines]
        ↓
[Causal & Systemic Analysis]
        ↓
[Risk Projection & Scenario Modeling]
        ↓
[Response & Remediation Planning]
        ↓
[Outputs & Reporting]
`

---

2. Node set (modules) with dependencies

2.1 Input & context layer

1. AssetInventoryLoader
   - Inputs: CMDB, cloud inventory, endpoint lists, app registry  
   - Outputs: AssetSet

2. ControlCatalogLoader
   - Inputs: NIST CSF, internal control library, tool catalog  
   - Outputs: ControlSet

3. PolicyBaselineLoader
   - Inputs: security policies, configuration baselines, SOPs  
   - Outputs: PolicyBaselineSet

4. ThreatModelLoader
   - Inputs: threat intel, attack patterns, risk register  
   - Outputs: ThreatModel

Edges:

- AssetInventoryLoader → AssetSet
- ControlCatalogLoader → ControlSet
- PolicyBaselineLoader → PolicyBaselineSet
- ThreatModelLoader → ThreatModel

These four feed almost everything downstream.

---

2.2 Signal intake layer

5. ConfigSignalCollector
   - Inputs: live configs, IaC, baseline diffs, PolicyBaselineSet
   - Outputs: ConfigSignalStream

6. OperationalSignalCollector
   - Inputs: patch status, backup logs, cert status, AssetSet
   - Outputs: OperationalSignalStream

7. TelemetrySignalCollector
   - Inputs: SIEM logs, EDR coverage, sensor maps, AssetSet
   - Outputs: TelemetrySignalStream

8. AttackSurfaceSignalCollector
   - Inputs: discovery scans, cloud APIs, AssetSet, ThreatModel
   - Outputs: AttackSurfaceSignalStream

Edges:

- PolicyBaselineSet → ConfigSignalCollector
- AssetSet → OperationalSignalCollector
- AssetSet → TelemetrySignalCollector
- AssetSet, ThreatModel → AttackSurfaceSignalCollector

---

2.3 Normalization & classification layer

9. SignalNormalizer
   - Inputs: ConfigSignalStream, OperationalSignalStream, TelemetrySignalStream, AttackSurfaceSignalStream
   - Outputs: NormalizedSignalStream

10. SignalClassifier
    - Inputs: NormalizedSignalStream
    - Outputs: typed signals:
      - MisconfigSignal
      - DriftSignal
      - VisibilityLossSignal
      - ControlFailureSignal
      - SurfaceExpansionSignal

Edges:

- ConfigSignalStream, OperationalSignalStream, TelemetrySignalStream, AttackSurfaceSignalStream → SignalNormalizer
- SignalNormalizer → SignalClassifier

---

2.4 Degradation event construction

11. DegradationEventBuilder
    - Inputs: classified signals, AssetSet, ControlSet
    - Outputs: DegradationEventStream  
      Each DegradationEvent has:
      - event_id
      - type (misconfig, drift, visibility_loss, etc.)
      - asset_id
      - control_id
      - timestamp
      - rawsignalrefs

12. ImpactSurfaceMapper
    - Inputs: DegradationEventStream, AssetSet, ThreatModel
    - Outputs: ImpactSurfaceSet (per event: affected systems, users, data classes)

Edges:

- SignalClassifier, AssetSet, ControlSet → DegradationEventBuilder
- DegradationEventBuilder, AssetSet, ThreatModel → ImpactSurfaceMapper

---

2.5 Control & framework mapping

13. ControlFrameworkMapper
    - Inputs: DegradationEventStream, ControlSet
    - Outputs: EventControlMapping  
      (NIST CSF function/category/subcategory, internal control IDs)

14. ComplianceImpactMapper
    - Inputs: EventControlMapping, PolicyBaselineSet
    - Outputs: ComplianceImpactSet (which policies/SOPs are violated or degraded)

Edges:

- DegradationEventStream, ControlSet → ControlFrameworkMapper
- ControlFrameworkMapper, PolicyBaselineSet → ComplianceImpactMapper

---

2.6 Scoring engines

15. ControlWeakeningScorer
    - Inputs: DegradationEventStream, EventControlMapping, ImpactSurfaceSet
    - Outputs: ControlWeakeningScoreSet

16. ExposureAmplificationScorer
    - Inputs: SurfaceExpansionSignal, ImpactSurfaceSet, ThreatModel
    - Outputs: ExposureAmplificationScoreSet

17. DetectionBlindnessScorer
    - Inputs: VisibilityLossSignal, TelemetrySignalStream, ImpactSurfaceSet
    - Outputs: DetectionBlindnessScoreSet

18. ProcessErosionScorer
    - Inputs: DriftSignal, PolicyBaselineSet, ComplianceImpactSet
    - Outputs: ProcessErosionScoreSet

19. SecurityDegradationIndexAggregator
    - Inputs: all score sets (15–18)
    - Outputs: SecurityDegradationIndex (SDI) per asset, per control, global

Edges:

- DegradationEventStream, EventControlMapping, ImpactSurfaceSet → ControlWeakeningScorer
- SurfaceExpansionSignal, ImpactSurfaceSet, ThreatModel → ExposureAmplificationScorer
- VisibilityLossSignal, TelemetrySignalStream, ImpactSurfaceSet → DetectionBlindnessScorer
- DriftSignal, PolicyBaselineSet, ComplianceImpactSet → ProcessErosionScorer
- ControlWeakeningScoreSet, ExposureAmplificationScoreSet, DetectionBlindnessScoreSet, ProcessErosionScoreSet → SecurityDegradationIndexAggregator

---

2.7 Causal & systemic analysis

20. RootCauseClassifier
    - Inputs: DegradationEventStream, score sets, OperationalSignalStream
    - Outputs: RootCauseSet (human error, misconfig, tool failure, process drift, design flaw)

21. SystemicWeaknessDetector
    - Inputs: RootCauseSet, EventControlMapping, ControlSet
    - Outputs: SystemicWeaknessSet (patterns across controls, teams, architectures)

22. TemporalDriftAnalyzer
    - Inputs: historical DegradationEventStream, historical score sets
    - Outputs: DriftTrajectorySet (time‑series of degradation)

Edges:

- DegradationEventStream, score sets, OperationalSignalStream → RootCauseClassifier
- RootCauseSet, EventControlMapping, ControlSet → SystemicWeaknessDetector
- Historical DegradationEventStream, Historical score sets → TemporalDriftAnalyzer

---

2.8 Risk projection & scenario modeling

23. DegradationTrajectoryModeler
    - Inputs: DriftTrajectorySet, SecurityDegradationIndex, SystemicWeaknessSet
    - Outputs: DegradationTrajectoryForecast

24. FailureCascadeModeler
    - Inputs: SystemicWeaknessSet, EventControlMapping, ThreatModel
    - Outputs: FailureCascadeGraph (how weakened controls can chain)

25. ExploitabilityForecaster
    - Inputs: DegradationTrajectoryForecast, FailureCascadeGraph, ThreatModel
    - Outputs: ExploitabilityForecastSet (likelihood & impact of exploitation)

Edges:

- DriftTrajectorySet, SecurityDegradationIndex, SystemicWeaknessSet → DegradationTrajectoryModeler
- SystemicWeaknessSet, EventControlMapping, ThreatModel → FailureCascadeModeler
- DegradationTrajectoryForecast, FailureCascadeGraph, ThreatModel → ExploitabilityForecaster

---

2.9 Response & remediation layer

26. ImmediateFixGenerator
    - Inputs: high‑severity events, score sets, ExploitabilityForecastSet
    - Outputs: ImmediateFixList (ranked, actionable items)

27. StructuralRemediationPlanner
    - Inputs: SystemicWeaknessSet, FailureCascadeGraph, PolicyBaselineSet
    - Outputs: StructuralRemediationPlan

28. ControlHardeningPlanner
    - Inputs: ControlWeakeningScoreSet, ThreatModel, ControlSet
    - Outputs: ControlHardeningPlan

29. VisibilityRestorationPlanner
    - Inputs: DetectionBlindnessScoreSet, TelemetrySignalStream, ImpactSurfaceSet
    - Outputs: VisibilityRestorationPlan

Edges:

- High‑severity score sets, ExploitabilityForecastSet → ImmediateFixGenerator
- SystemicWeaknessSet, FailureCascadeGraph, PolicyBaselineSet → StructuralRemediationPlanner
- ControlWeakeningScoreSet, ThreatModel, ControlSet → ControlHardeningPlanner
- DetectionBlindnessScoreSet, TelemetrySignalStream, ImpactSurfaceSet → VisibilityRestorationPlanner

---

2.10 Outputs & reporting

30. RiskDashboardAssembler
    - Inputs: SDI, forecasts, plans, ImpactSurfaceSet
    - Outputs: dashboards, APIs

31. ReportGenerator
    - Inputs: all major artifacts (events, scores, causes, plans)
    - Outputs: human‑readable reports (exec, ops, audit)

Edges:

- SecurityDegradationIndex, ExploitabilityForecastSet, ImpactSurfaceSet, remediation plans → RiskDashboardAssembler
- All upstream artifacts → ReportGenerator
